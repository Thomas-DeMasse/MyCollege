Expenses

Track Your Expenses.

Expenses represent the funds we use to cover everyday needs, like bills and groceries, as well as discretionary items, shaping our financial health and budget.


Tracking expenses empowers individuals by revealing spending patterns, aiding budgeting, and enabling informed financial choices.

How to Track Expenses?

  1. Select the date
  2. Press Add Button
  3. Click on the form created on the right side to inpit
  4. Fill the item name
  5. Choose the category
  6. Choose the amount
  7. Select Submit
Now you are on right path to keep track of eveything.
